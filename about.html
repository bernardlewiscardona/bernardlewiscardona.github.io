Who Are We?

About Paw Project

Paw Project is a registered non-profit organization which is dedicated to the protection and the promotion of humane treatment of all animals. We are a volunteer-based organization.

Our main work is to educate and disseminate information concerning animal welfare, animal care, animal control, and other animal-related concerns. Paw Project campaigns actively against animal cruelty and pet neglect, including activities like dogfights and animals being used for entertainment.

Rescue and Shelter

Paw Project is dedicated to giving animals who are in extreme pain a second chance at life. We rescue strays who are suffering in the streets with no one to take care of them. We also rescue pets who are abused, neglected, or abandoned by their owners. Occasionally, we financially assist owners who love their pets but cannot afford treatment for painful diseases.

On June 6, 2021, Paw Project achieved a major milestone: the Paw Project Animal Shelter was completed. It now houses over 100 rescues who have fully recovered and are up for adoption. The shelter is only a temporary home; each rescue deserves special love and attention that only a family can provide. Nevertheless, we do our best to keep them happy and healthy while they wait for their forever home.
