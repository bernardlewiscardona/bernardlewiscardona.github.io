What We Do?

Rescue

We give animals a second chance in life. These are stray animals who are all alone and have no one to look after them. We give them treatment, check-up and trained them.

Vaccine

Every pet is entitled to a long and healthy life. Vaccinations are one way we promote responsible pet ownership.

Shelter

We have a facility for rescued dogs and cats. They are fed three times a day, cleaned, and have plenty of space to play. Currently we have 100+ dogs and cats in our facility.

Adoption

When you adopt, you are transforming more than one life. We work hard to find loving homes for our rescues. Adopters are strictly screened to make sure that they can give our rescues a better life.

Spay & Neuter

One of Paw Project's objectives is to achieve a stray-free Philippines. We advocate for spaying and neutering.

Frequently Asked Questions

Applicants must have an account first, submit the requirements and go through a screening process to ensure that our rescued animals go to loving homes. The process includes a meet a greet with your prospective pet, three (3) separate visits to bond and get to know your chosen animal, and finally, an ocular inspection of your home.

Paw Project does NOT adopt owned pets. The 100+ animals we have at the shelter are all rescued from cruelty and neglect, and are waiting to be adopted. If you need to give up your pet for some reason, please consider other options.

Responsible pet ownership is a lifetime commitment, which is why we don’t take in abandoned pets. We can assist in rehoming your pet by advertising within our networks , NOT by admitting your pet at the shelter, and ONLY if conditions and qualifications are met.
