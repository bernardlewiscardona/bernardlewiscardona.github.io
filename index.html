What We Do?

Rescue

We give animals a second chance in life. These are stray animals who are all alone and have no one to look after them. We give them treatment, check-up and trained them.

Vaccine

Every pet is entitled to a long and healthy life. Vaccinations are one way we promote responsible pet ownership.

Shelter

We have a facility for rescued dogs and cats. They are fed three times a day, cleaned, and have plenty of space to play. Currently we have 100+ dogs and cats in our facility.

Adoption

When you adopt, you are transforming more than one life. We work hard to find loving homes for our rescues. Adopters are strictly screened to make sure that they can give our rescues a better life.
