Please donate and help save our shelter animals

Every year, the Paw Project cares for around 300 dogs and cats rescued from cruelty or neglect. The costs of treating and rehabilitating these animals, as well as running the shelter and clinic, are funded solely by donations.

Donate In Kind

Your donations can be dropped off at the shelter. If you wish to have a tour of the shelter, please schedule your visit in advance. Otherwise, we can only receive your donations at the reception area. Thank you!

Cats & Dogs Wish List

  • Dog food (kibble and canned)
  • Catfood (kibble and canned)
  • Crates, carriers or cages
  • Pet diapers and wee wee pads

  • Dog and cat treats
